Terra, limassol

Our design for the Terra project consists of 10 luxury apartments, on top of a double-height commercial plinth on the coast of Limassol, Cyprus. With the design we focus on blending an elegant and unique tower into a recreational beach area, close to the sea and part of the holiday and entertainment zone of the city.
